## Ukrainian n-gram dictionaries and word lists

`Rekey/Resources/nonexistent*-uk.txt`, `Rekey/Resources/knownwords-uk.txt`,
`Rekey.Tests/Resources/words-uk.txt`

Generated by [tools/Rekey.DictGen](tools/Rekey.DictGen) in this repository.
The generated lists are statistical facts about the Ukrainian language (letter
combinations that never occur in Ukrainian words); no source text is reproduced.
The corpora used as evidence:

- **ParaCrawl v9, Ukrainian monolingual** — released under
  [CC0 1.0](https://creativecommons.org/publicdomain/zero/1.0/) ("no rights reserved")
  by the [ParaCrawl project](https://paracrawl.eu/); obtained via
  [OPUS](https://opus.nlpl.eu/ParaCrawl.php).
- **UA-GEC** (corrected/target texts) — © Grammarly,
  [github.com/grammarly/ua-gec](https://github.com/grammarly/ua-gec), licensed under
  [CC BY 4.0](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/).
  Citation: Syvokon et al., *UA-GEC: Grammatical Error Correction and Fluency Corpus
  for the Ukrainian Language* (2023).

No data from CC BY-NC-SA sources (e.g. brown-uk/dict_uk) is used.
